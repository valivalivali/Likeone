# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 5, 2024**

## Overview

Likeone ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Likeone mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

When you use Likeone, we collect the following information:

### Account Information
- **Name**: Your display name shown on your profile
- **User ID**: A unique identifier from Sign in with Apple

### User Content
- **Videos**: Video profiles you upload to the app

### Purchase Information
- **Purchase History**: Records of in-app purchases (likes and exchanges)

### Usage Data
- **Product Interaction**: How you interact with the app (profile views, matches, messages)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:
- Providing and maintaining the app
- Displaying your profile to other users
- Enabling matches and messaging
- Processing in-app purchases

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase and Cloudflare. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete your account and associated data at any time
- Request a copy of your data
- Update your profile information within the app

## Children's Privacy

Likeone is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
